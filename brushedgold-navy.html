The Originals Brushed Gold / Navy

The Brushed Gold / Navy is a beautiful timepiece with a strap made of the finest leather. It is a part of our Originals collection, one of the most popular watch collections worldwide. The wide strap and large face allow the wearer to make a statement. Without the number lines on the face, the Brushed Gold / Navy is both simple but grand. It can be worn with a casual or classy look.
