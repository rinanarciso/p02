Dexterton x Kathryn Chandria

Fall 2016

Dexterton partnered up with fashion designer Kathryn Chandria to create beautiful leather time tellers. This collection was featured in Lou Lou Magazine in an article written by John Daniels.

Made with the finest leather, the Dexterton x Kathryn collection is sure to last long and stay trendy.

This collection is available in four seasonal treatments. They are perfect to wear anywhere and for any occasion.

The Dexterton x Kathryn collection is now available in all Dexterton stores and at dexterton.com.
