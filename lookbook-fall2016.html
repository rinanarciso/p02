Dexterton x Kathryn Chandria

Fall 2016

Dexterton partnered up with fashion designer Kathryn Chandria to create beautiful leather time tellers. This collection was featured in Lou Lou Magazine in an article written by John Daniels.

The Classics Rose Gold / Grey Leather

The Originals Polished Steel / Mineral Green

Kathryn envisioned a photo collection to show her pieces against neutral colors to draw attention to the trendy collection.

The Originals Polished Silver / Blush

The watches are available in many classic colors, such as Blush, Mineral Green, and Powder Blue, and they look especially great with denim or light-colored clothing.

The Originals Polished Silver / Blush

The Classics Classic Silver / Black

Made with the finest leather, the Dexterton x Kathryn collection is sure to last long and stay trendy. The collection is now available at dexterton.com.
