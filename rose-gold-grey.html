The Classics Rose Gold / Grey Leather

The Rose Gold / Grey Leather is one of our timeless pieces, wearable for both men and women. It is a classic timepiece that was curated professionally and with every detail in mind. With a narrow body and a clean-lined dial designed with a single subdial that counts seconds, ‘The Classic’ compliments both casual and sophisticated looks. The robust strap is made from supple Italian leather with beige nubuck lining and finished with a tapered stitch.
